Pandora, Unbridled Bringer of Hope

A girl created by the gods of Olympus. Pandora mistakenly opens a box that unleashes calamity into the world. Her curiosity and sense of responsibility set her on a quest to undo her blunder. After some time has passed, Pandora is confronted by a maniacal monster that is the embodiment of calamity. It's at this time that Mopey, the embodiment of loneliness and sadness, throws himself into the box, awakening a new power in Pandora.
