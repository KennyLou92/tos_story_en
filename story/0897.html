Tang Hsue-Jian

“In my eyes, Jing Tian and great martial artisan are like the binarity of the sun and the moon. When have you seen the two appear together?” As the granddaughter of Tang School’s chief Tang Kun, Tang Hsue-Jian had become spoiled and capricious by her family. Unfortunately, when Tang Kun passed away from sickness, she was ostracized despite her position as chief. Tired of the conflicts within Tang School, all Hsue-Jian could do was to journey together with Jing Tian, the annoying Yong An Pawn Shop staff. Their relationship seemed to be predestined...
