Wily Vulpes - Jiang Xiang

Date of Birth: 26 Jan A three-tailed vixen and the descendant of Jiang Ziya and Daji. She is the current representative of gods. Having a rather playful and energetic personality, she is an optimist full of compassion. She is told to be an extraordinary Xian general by the Cereus Prophesier. She excels in different kinds of seduction enchantment and Xian arts.
