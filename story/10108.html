Lifelong Covenant - He Jin

Citizen of the Xianglon Empire. The Deputy Chief of the Starmage’s Wellings branch. A descendant of the He’s Clan that has served the Runedragons for generations. He Jin is constantly worried that Ke Hun may get into trouble due to his slack personality so he follows Ke Hun closely like a babysitter. His colleagues in the branch nicknamed him “Ke Hun’s Mount” for he often serves as a moving tool under Ke Hun’s order.
