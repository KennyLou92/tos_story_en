Jiang Yun-Fan, Prince of Devils

“I will finish what I have promised. Also, I won’t let anyone die!” Jiang Yun-Fan was a man of his word. For the woman he loved, he was even willing to give up his life. Yet, reality was harsh, and tragedy seemed to follow him. His long-lost father sacrificed his own life for Jiang Yun-Fan upon their reconciliation. His beloved finally accepted him, but she soon perished. As all became memory, Jiang Yun-Fan and his dear pet defended the seal on Shushan as he lived a life of reminiscence — one where he faced the lost of all those closest to him in silence...
