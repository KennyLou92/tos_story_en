Glowing Belligerence - Fushi

Fushi the Featherette has a strong personality, full of confidence and even can be called narcissism. He likes to compete with others. Fushi insists on following when he knows Luomo wants to assist Feiyin. Later, he meets Iyo in Yamatai and desires for a battle with her. In the course of time, he develops a liking to Iyo.
