Kittan & King Kittan

The leader of “Black Siblings” the Beastmen hunters, also the eldest male member. Kittan perhaps is even more hotheaded and irritable than Kamina? Therefore he is always competing with Kamina. Simple, impulsive yet indomitable, he is the pilot of “King Kittan”. The giant horn on the head allows him to use the "King Kittan Stinger" ultimate ability.
