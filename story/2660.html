Queen of Yamatai - Himiko

The queen of Yamatai. Himiko possesses the bearing of the queen and wisdom but also retains child-like innocence and curiosity. Nightmare keeps haunting her since her mother is missing. In order not to let the people of Yamatai and her friends worry about her, Himiko always hides her weakness. One day, the unknown attacked the magic barrier of the kingdom. To avoid inflicting wars again, Himiko sent a commission to Starmage for investigation.
