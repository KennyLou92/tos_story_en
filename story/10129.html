All Might

The hero who bore the title of the world's Symbol of Peace. He was also the eighth holder of One For All, the transferable Quirk that can stockpile powers. After the fight with his arch-nemesis, All For One, All Might used up all his power and retired as the No.1 hero. Living on as a U.A. teacher, he is now training and guiding Deku, his successor of One For All.
