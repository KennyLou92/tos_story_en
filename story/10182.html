Sougo Okita

A good-looking man with a baby face and a sadistic soul. Being the 1st Division Captain of the Shinsengumi, he always slacks off on his job. He is the kind of person who causes a chorus of complaints from the neighborhood by firing his own bazooka inconsiderately during work time and brings disgrace to the Shinsengumi. Perhaps he has a grudge against Hijikata-he always aims for Hijikata’s life no matter if he has a chance or not. He has a hobby of watching women’s wrestling, possibly because the ugly faces of women during a battle tickles his sadistic soul.
