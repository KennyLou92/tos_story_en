Damian & Emile & Ewen

Damian is the second son of Loid's target, Donovan Desmond, and Anya's classmate. Although he is extremely proud and audacious, he can be a rather determined boy who just wants to be acknowledged by his father. He is trying to become an Imperial Scholar for some reason. Emile and Ewen are the classmates of Anya and also the followers of Damian.
