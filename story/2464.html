Ludoshel the Leader of the Four Archangels

One of the four archangels of the Goddess Clan. During the Holy War 3000 years ago, he sealed the Demon Clan by sacrificing his body. Later in the present, he uses Margaret, who is kidnapped because of Gilthunder, to revive. To wipe out the Demon Clan, he persuades the Holy Knights to join “Stigma”, and he allies with the “Seven Deadly Sins” led by Elizabeth.
