Whirling Shadow - Heifeng Guai

A monster that possess a savage and merciless disposition, excelling in toying with the hearts and minds of living beings. She has a fondness for adorning herself with dazzling jewelry. Despite her imposing and voluptuous physique, she exhibits remarkable agility and speed that resembles a fleeting gust of wind, which earns her the nickname of Heifeng Guai, the Black Wind Demon.
