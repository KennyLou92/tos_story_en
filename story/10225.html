Naraku, God of the Abyss

A dark entity sealed deep within a forbidden prison. Having destroyed all existing material worlds, Naraku had exhausted her power and fallen into a deep sleep. When sensing the birth of a new material world, she awakens from her slumber and descends upon the world to once again return everything to the void. The "Eye of Destruction" is bound to the pattern on Naraku's forehead, and anything caught in it is destroyed.
