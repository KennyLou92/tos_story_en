KAMEN RIDER BLACK

“Gorgom” is the dark society leading by “Creation King”. Kotaro Minami and his best friend, Nobuhiko Akizuki, are chosen as candidates for the next “Creation King”. Kotaro Minami forced to turn into the Century King, “Black Sun”, by cyborg surgery, breaks away at the minute of brain surgery and embarks on the fight against Gorgom. Kotaro fights as justice soldier, “Kamen Rider Black”, and wrecks the plot of Gorgom by battling with numerous Kaijin and antagonist, and Nobuhiko as the Gorgom soldier, “Shadow Moon”.
