Han Ling-Sha

“What do you mean by ‘thief’! I am a great ‘bandit’. It is ‘bandit’! Got it?” 　 Han Ling-Sha, with her bright personality and wits, was actually a travelling tomb robber who carried the destiny of her entire family. She would rob the rich to help the poor but would not boast of her accomplishments. Despite her young age, she had already cultivated great technique in Feng Shui, which she used to breakthrough the traps. Upon learning of the Sword Saints legends, she went to investigate; it was there she met Yun Tian-He. With that, her fate was changed forever...
