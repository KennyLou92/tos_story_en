Bakugo Katsuki

The childhood friend of Deku. Being superior in intelligence, physical strength and battle sense, Bakugo is arrogant and has a strong determination to become “No.1 Hero” that drives him to make constant self-improvement. His Quirk is Explosion, which allows him to secrete nitroglycerin-like sweat from his palms and ignite it on command to create strong explosions.
