Shinsuke Takasugi

A dangerous character who leads the Kiheitai, an extremist Joui faction, and plotted terrorism out of his hatred against the world that is controlled by the Amanto. He is an old friend of Gintoki and Katsura but their friendship ended after the Joui War until today. Like Gintoki and Katsura, he is also a charismatic man that attracts people.
