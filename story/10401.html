Zeke Yeager & Beast Titan

Zeke Yaeger is the Commander of Marley's Warriors. He is the son of Grisha, and also the paternal half-brother of Eren. He is the holder of the ""Beast Titan"". Beast Titan is a Titan covered in beast-like hair. The Beast Titans have the power to exhibit various animal-like traits and Zeke's Beast Titan shows the traits of a primate. For example, it can make long-distance throws with its long arms. In addition, it can turn the Subjects of Ymir who have been given its user's spinal fluid into Pure Titans with a roar.
