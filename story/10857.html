Emilia & Pack

She is kind-hearted and caring, but for some reason, she refuses to acknowledge that. Accompanied by a cat-like spirit named Pack, she is one of the Royal Candidates for the throne of the Kingdom of Lugunica. Pack is a spirit whom Emilia is contracted with.
