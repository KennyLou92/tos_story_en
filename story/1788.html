Genkai

Genkai is an expert of Spirit Wave and the master of Yusuke. When using the Spirit Wave, her body cells will be rejuvenated, which temporarily reverses to the form in which she is at her peak in life. Genkai is killed during the Dark Tournament after a powerful blow from Younger Toguro, but subsequently brought back afterwards. Genkai was one of team members who won the Dark Tournament 50 years ago.
