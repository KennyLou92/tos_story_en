Takeru Takaishi & Patamon & Hikari Yagami & Gatomon

Takeru Takaishi is a 2nd grader student. He is Yamato Ishida’s little brother but the two live apart because of their parents’ divorce. He has a kind and innocent personality. Patamon is Takeru’s Partner Digimon, who also has an unsophisticated mind as Takeru. Hikari Yagami is a 2nd grader, who is the cheerful young sibling of Yagami Taichi. She can hear some mysterious “voices” that no one can hear. Gatomon is Hikari’s Partner Digimon, who has a mature and cool personality.
