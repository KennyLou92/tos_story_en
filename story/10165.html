The Savior - Enoch

The savior who liberated the power of the origin in the old age and the principal of Charlouette, the magic agency. Having a kind soul, he loves helping others. Although he is sometimes indecisive, he always makes precise decisions at critical junctures. After liberating the power of the origin, he was committed to bringing about peace among the seven races with his companions in the magic agency and finally established Heptara Unies after a century of efforts.
