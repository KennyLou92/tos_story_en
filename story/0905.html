Jing Tian

“In the future... I want to open a pawn shop, and collect a lot of antiques. Also, I’d like to cultivate my Xian techniques and become a true Sword Saint. I also want to explore the martial world, and also...” Jing Tian worked at the famous Yong An Pawn Shop. He was not only skilled in the assessment of antiques and jewelry, but also in trading. Nevertheless, he dreamt of Sword Saint-hood and becoming the renowned Sword Saint of Shushan. It was this passion that drove him onto his eventful adventures...
