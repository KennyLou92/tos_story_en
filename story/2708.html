KAMEN RIDER DEN-O

“Imagine” is the invader coming from the future. They attempt to change the future by making changes in the past. Ryotaro Nogami, a young man with bad luck, found a mysterious ticket, “Rider Pass”. He encounters a time traveling train, “Den-Liner” and is possessed by Momotaros. However, surprisingly, it turns out that he has a unique quality which enables him to resist Imagine and transform into Kamen Rider Den-O. He cooperates with Imagine and fights as a warrior, Den-O.
