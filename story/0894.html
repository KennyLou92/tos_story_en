Righteous Heroine - Lin Yue-Ru

‘Dear Hsiao-Yao, who am I in your heart?’ Ever since she started traveling with Li Hsiao-Yao, Lin Yue-Ru had been happy with her daily life. She loved her adventures by his side, friendships with the people who meet on the road, and the cultivation of her marital skills. Yet, their relationship was one of underlying tensions until that day when Yue-Ru’s father told him to take care of her. Yue-Ru honestly told him her sentiments. Touched, Li Hsiao-Yao promised her that when he had finished all this, he would take her on a leisurely journey, and taste various delicacies. but it seemed that the fulfillment of the promise was not included in their destiny...
