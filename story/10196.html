Prince Hata

The prince of planet Oukoku. He is known for his infatuation with exotic animals instead of cute pets. People call him “Prince Baka”. Every time he brings a rare animal to Kabukicho,there will be chaos. He is a troubling prince that even though he has expressed a tendency to introspect about his deed, he always forgets it and soon does it again. The thing that looks like a tentacle on his head is his best feature but it is frequently ripped off by others.
