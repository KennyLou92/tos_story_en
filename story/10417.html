Caged Albino - Snow

An ethereally beautiful girl who becomes a noble demon’s toy because of Marcus Karnstein, who forces her to make a slave pact. Innocent and kind-hearted, she takes care of the enslaved children who are younger than her. As her power awakens in an accident, she gains the ability to distort anything. However, her new power soon catches Marcus’ attention, who threatens to kill all her friends if she doesn’t work as an assassin for him.
