Heir of Excalibur - Frederick

Date of Birth: 1 Aug The deputy representative of humanity. The fifth prince of Verdilles and the descendant of Arthur Pendragon. He seems arrogant and conceited most of the time, but deep down he is a kind and innocent young man. He is nicknamed “angry kitten” by Cilovic due to his short temper. He volunteered himself to be the deputy representative out of admiration for Tsukishita Hitomi, the representative of humanity. As the wielder of Excalibur, he is a skillful swordsman who is able to perform the Covenant of Blood.
