Luna & Artemis & Diana

Being Usagi’s partner, Luna is a black female cat with a crescent mark on its forehead. It has been supporting Usagi and the others since it awakened them as Sailor Guardians. As Minako’s partner, Artemis is a white male cat with a crescent mark on its forehead. It awakened Minako to become a Sailor Guardian. Diana is a kitten that is also the future daughter of Luna and Artemis. With a reliable personality and courteous behavior, she often takes care of Chibi-Usa, who also sees it as an understanding friend.
