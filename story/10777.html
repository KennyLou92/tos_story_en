Saintess of Love - Aria

She was once betrothed to Prince Uther while serving as the royal sorceress of Verdilles. To save Prince Uther, who fell under a demon’s curse, she willingly tethered herself to the holy grail and gave up her identity as Aria, living on as the saintess of the kingdom. She even asked the holy grail to erase Uther’s memories of her to spare him the grief and sorrow.
