Colossus Titan (Armin)

A Colossus Titan that Armin transforms into. It is 60 meters tall, even taller than the three Walls on Paradis Island. Its user can create large-scale explosions with the blasts of wind produced during their transformation. Despite its sluggish movement, it can launch destructive attacks with its gigantic body and emit hot steam by burning its muscles to repel any enemies in close proximity.
