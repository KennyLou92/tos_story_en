General of Spiral King - Guame the Immovable

One of the Four Generals of the Spiral King, also known as "Guame the Immovable". He is an armadillo-type Beastman, and seems to be acquainted with the Spiral King for a long time. Sometimes Guame is quite offensive, so even Cytomander will show respect to him. Guame owns the flagship "Dai-Gundo", and is also the pilot of Gunmen "Gember".
