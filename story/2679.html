Enmu

One of the Twelve Kizuki of Muzan Kibutsuji, he is very sadistic in personality, and enjoys the pain and suffering of humans. After the Lower demons are killed, Enmu is the only one who survived because of Kibutsuji’s appreciation. Compared with Rui the Lower Five who had a tough battle with Tanjiro in Mount Natagumo, Enmu is Lower One who has a higher rank than him, and is given Muzan Kibutsuji’s blood.
