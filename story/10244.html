Yamato Ishida & Gabumon

Yamato Ishida is a 5th grader. Having a cool demeanor, he is rather clumsy with words but he is a deeply caring person who pays meticulous attention to both his companions’ physical and mental conditions. He later learns that it takes mutual support to make true friendships through his interaction with Gabumon and his quarrels with Taichi. Gabumon is Yamato’s Partner Digimon, who has a gentle and compassionate personality. He likes it when Yamato plays the harmonica. Gabumon can digivolve into Garurumon, the warrior of speed and power.
