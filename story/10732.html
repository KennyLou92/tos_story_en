Magisterial Poise - Tadayuki Kamo

A citizen of Tōwa Teito. He is the warden of the prison of the Court of Internation Justice. Although he has a slothful and unkempt demeanor of a man who doesn’t seem to care about anything, he has everything under his control. Proficient in onmyōdō, he uses his power to suppress the most heinous criminals in the prison.
