Enslaved Guardian - Xiao Yu

She is originally Huangdi’s inner demon but she is later enslaved, becoming a guardian spirit of a secret realm he has made. Despite having the same appearance as Huangdi’s sister-in-law whom he once had a crush on, she has a rather mischievous nature that starkly contrasts with the original’s gentle and kind-hearted personality. She takes pleasure in playing pranks on others
