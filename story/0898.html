Tang Hsue-Jian, Flower of a Goddess

“The moment I think of... leaving your side, I will suddenly feel pain in my heart... It feels like my heart... is about to crack; this must be... yearning.” Hsue-Jian who was forged by Goddess Xi Yao with a celestial fruit journeyed by Jing Tian’s side, experiencing the pain of separation and death. She had fallen for this boisterous fellow before she discovered she actually had an unbreakable bond with Jing Tian, a recineration of Fei Peng. The adventures the two shared was unforgettable for Hsue-Jian. In this incarnation, would their relationship blossom?
