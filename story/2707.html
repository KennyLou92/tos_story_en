MASKED RIDER 1

Takeshi Hongo is captured by the Secret Society, Shocker, who attempts to take over the Earth and to enforce cyborg operation on his body. But Hongo Takeshi breaks away at the last minute of brain surgery and made up his mind to fight as Kamen Rider #1 to save the Earth. With Ichimonji Hayato, Kamen Rider #2, they together stand against Shocker and Gel-Shocker and the epic battle between “Kamen Rider”and “the Secret Society” begins!
