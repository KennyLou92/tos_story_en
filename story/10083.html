Aquarius Camus (Surplice)

The Gold Saint of Aquarius and the Master of Cygnus Hyoga. Considering Hyoga as his pupil, Camus locked him inside the Freezing Coffin in the Sanctuary Chapter. With the lowest possible temperature, the Absolute Zero, his punch can freeze every living creature. Although Camus is usually calm, he has a soft spot for his pupil and a kind heart.
