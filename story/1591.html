Ice Doll of Freeze - Kula Diamond

Kula Diamond is a being created by N.E.S.T.S. She was designed to exterminate the cartel's traitor, K´. Kula has an impressive control over ice which was designed to counter K´’s flames and is capable of freezing water in the atmosphere in a blink. When using her powers, Kula's hair becomes blue and her eyes turn magenta. Kula lives with K' after the collapse of N.E.S.T.S.
