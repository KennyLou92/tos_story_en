KAMEN RIDER ZERO-ONE

At the center of a city, there is a skyscraper of the Hiden Intelligence’s headquarter, the big leading company serving a society for any of high technology such as artificial intelligence and robotics. A young man, Hiden Aruto, the last person who cares about the news of the CEO’s death, is rushing on his bike to an amusement park to appear on a stage as a comedian. He is the one who transforms into Kamen Rider Zero-One.
