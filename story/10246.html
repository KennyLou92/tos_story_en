Mimi Tachikawa & Palmon

Mimi Tachikawa is a 4th grader. Although she sometimes comes off as a spoiled preppy who does things her own way, she is initially a candid and kind girl. She once left Taichi’s squad for she could not stand seeing so many Digimons die protecting them. Palmon is Mimi’s Partner Digimon, who is a bit faint-hearted but it has a kind and easygoing spirit. It can digivolve into Togemon and fight with its hardened thorns.
