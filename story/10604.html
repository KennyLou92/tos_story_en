Oriental Heiress - Hitomi Tsukishita

The current Teiki of Tōwa Teito, who holds the dual role of being the representative of humans. Her legs are paralyzed due to a peculiar ailment that confines her to a wheelchair. With an exquisite countenance and a keen intellect, she commands mastery over both magic formulas and onmyōdō, seamlessly blending their powers in the heat of battle. Her astute perception of human nature grants her the ability to unravel others’ mind, occasionally revealing a competitive spirit that adds an intriguing facet to her character. She is a force to be reckoned with, never to be underestimated. She has a unique friendship with Cilovic, the Chief of the Heptara Unies Department of Security.
