The Immortal Sage Penglai

A high school girl who possesses the power of the mythical land of Penglai. She is respected at Penglai Girl's School for possessing an unpresuming demeanor, despite her excellent grades and good looks. Her eyes are different colors, proof of the power of Penglai in her body. Her words and actions carry a certain weight due to this power. When she must fight with all of her strength, she beckons her Striker into the land of Penglai. When releasing her power, she transforms into a celestial maiden, carrying in her palm the fabled jeweled branch of Penglai.
