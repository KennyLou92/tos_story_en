Tang Yu-Rou

“I’ll stay beside you no matter what happens...” Tang Yu-Rou, daughter of Tang Feng, was a gentle young lady of an eminent family. She was so kind-hearted that even a withering flower would upset her. Her pneuma reminded one of a lovely rainy day with soft breeze. Yu-Rou was compassionate, considerate but also tough. Though she was born in the Sect of Purification, she was a disciple of Cao Gu, one of the Seven Saints of Shushan. She followed her master to practice art of healing. One day when she was searching for herbs, she encountered the most important man in her life — Jiang Yun-Fan...
