Two For All: The Power of Unity

The idol group formed in the anime "MSonic! D'Artagnyan's Rise to Fame". The group consists of the energetic D'Artagnyan and the hardworking Shion. Two For All performs original songs live in cyberspace. Their ardent desire is for everyone who listens to their music to unite and share joy, hence the band name, "Two For All". Through their kind voices, their songs reverberate throughout the world, becoming the bridge that connects all listeners.
