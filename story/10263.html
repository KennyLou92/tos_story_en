Numemon ＆ Sukamon ＆ Chuumon

Numemon is a Champion level Virus type Mollusk Digimon. With a low combat power, it has a killing technique called Nume-Sludge. Sukamon is a Champion level Virus type Abnormal Digimon. It is always seen with Chuumon because of its nonexistent intelligence. Chuumon is a Rookie level Virus type Beast Digimon. It always stays with Sukamon as the latter’s brain with the strong bonds of friendship developed between them.
