Barbules of Grace - Luomo

Luomo the Featherette is not good at and not used to expressing himself. Therefore, he has always been reticent. He doesn’t like humans as they’re too sentimental, so he’s seldom in human form. In the past, he was assisted by Feiyin. That’s why he came to Yamatai to return the favor. He likes being quiet, and finds the talkative Chimeng annoying.
