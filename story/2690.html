Soaring Aspiration - Jichen

Jichen was once the strongest in the petrogon clan, but lost the title after his Lumin was damaged. Despite this, his power should not be underestimated. Mature and reliable, he’s a kind, erudite person who earns the deep trust of the clan. The damaged Lumin causes him unable to last long in his human form, so he’s been actively seeking the cure.
