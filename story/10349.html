Super Sailor Pluto

Super Sailor Pluto is the Guardian of Transition, protected by Pluto, the Planet of Space-Time. Holding one of the three “Talismans”—Garnet Orb, she is a guardian of the Door of Space-Time. She has been taking care of the team with tenderness. In her civilian form as Setsuna Meiou, she is a grown woman veiled in mysteries.
