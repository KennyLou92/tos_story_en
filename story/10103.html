Dreamy Miser - Lazurite

Date of Birth: 19 May A Minerelf working in the Intelligence Unit of Heptara Unies. She has a huge obsession with money that she keeps inducing Cilovic, the president, to give her a pay raise with various methods. Unfortunately, she hasn’t gotten what she wants so far. She is more bark than bite. Although she’s always complaining about how hard her work is, she always does her job diligently.
