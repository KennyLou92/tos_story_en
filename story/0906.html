Jing Tian the General Reincarnated

“I’m Jing Tian. My reputation precedes me...” As a reincarnation of Fei Peng the mighty general, Jing Tian had hoped to easily be the best Sword Saint of his generation, enjoying the respect of all and earning a fortune. Later, the appearance of Chong Lou prompted him on his journey. Not only did he learn about his previous life, but he also prompted a continuation of the fated links between himself, Chong Lou and Long Kui. The wheels of fate has started spinning.
