KAMEN RIDER GENM ZOMBIE ACTION GAMER LEVEL X-0

Kuroto Dan transforms into this form after the level up. The Gashacon Breaker (Blade Mode) is mainly used in the battle, and the invincible mode of the Hyper Muteki Gashat is launched in the battle with the Kamen Rider Cronus Chronicle Gamer. In order to develop a vaccine for the Gamedeus virus and the Doctor Mighty XX Gashat, there is also a form that assists Kamen Rider Lazer Turbo in a death match.
