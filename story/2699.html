KAMEN RIDER KUUGA MIGHTY FORM

Once upon a time, a battle between the mysterious tribes, “Grongi” and the warrior, “Kuuga” broke out. In 2000, with an ancient belt, Yusuke Godai, an adventurer, embarkes on the battle to save people from Grongi which keeps coming after him. Harder the battle gets, more he evolves. To save everyone’s smile, Godai fights the desperate battle with a cop, Kaoru Ichijo.
