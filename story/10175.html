Sadaharu

A pet of the Yorozuya which is slightly large in size. Kagura adopted it after it had been abandoned. Its enormous body and superpower often bring danger to its owner. Although it seems to be just an enormous dog, it is actually an “Inugami” that guards the Dragon's Lair. Its true power can be restored by feeding it with red fruit (strawberries) and goat blood (or milk).
