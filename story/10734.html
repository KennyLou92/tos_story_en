Iniquity Decimation - Ruth

A citizen of the United States of Asgard, a nation located on the northern Tarones. She is a senior judge at the Court of International Justice. Prudent and judicious, she excels in handling complex cases. While she seems aloof, she cares deeply for her colleagues. As an expert in magic, she can use various magic formulas, many of which are focused on binding and tracking that are incredibly helpful when it comes to apprehending criminals.
