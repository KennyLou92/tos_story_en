Final Impact - Yashiro Nanakase

Yashiro Nanakase is one of the Four Heavenly Kings of Orochi. Blessed by Orochi, Yashiro is able to control the earth. He enjoys traveling around alone to meet other descendants. He once formed a band with Shermie and Chris; their band has a grudge against Iori Yagami's band. This motivated him to team up with Shermie and Chris again to compete with Iori Yagami in the King of Fighters tournament.
