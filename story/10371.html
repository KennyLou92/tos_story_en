Cessation of Ones - Una

Date of Birth: 6 Jun The spirit born from the unprecedented dragonwares that Dolak, the dragonary craft master, created with the corpse of Azathoth. Una is the name given to her. Short-tempered and competitive, she is a bold and fierce troublemaker who always needs Nulla or Dolak to clean up her messes. As a sword dragonware, she is excellent at offense, both short-distance and long-distance attacks.
