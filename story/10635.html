Catastrophic Outgrowth - Unknown Creature

In ancient times, Ubo, the primordial dragon, shattered galaxies to fashion an ancient relic known as the Inifinite Web, capable of transmitting power across vast distances, hoping to draw the twisted energy within Daoloth, but little did they anticipate the magnitude of Daoloth’s powers would surpass the web’s capabilities and even gave birth to an unusual creature. To seal the ominous power, Daoloth buried the web along with the unusual lifeform in the core of a celestial body, where it remained hidden for ages until the Holy Gods unearthed it.
