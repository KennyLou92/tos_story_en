McCarthy, Father of Mechs

McCarthy is a renowned 23rd century scientist. He's obsessed with science and his creations in artificial intelligence can be seen in manufacturing, commerce, and the military. To create an AI with emotion and sentience had long been his dream, but no matter what did, there always seemed to be something missing. Then he rescued the wounded Rosalind, who had journeyed through space-time. For the first time, McCarthy found himself interested in something other than science. He started to understand what emotion was, and so "it" evolved...
