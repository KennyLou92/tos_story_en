Team Dai-Gurren Leader - Simon & GURREN LAGANN

Simon reconstructed Kamina City in Teppelin the ruined royal capital and took on the position of Supreme Commander to handle daily public affairs. Although the trait of being wimpish still remains, he has grown up to be more masculine in terms of the appearance and emotions. "Gurren Lagann" is a gigantic, combined Gunmen of "Gurren" and "Lagann". It keeps the sunglasses boomerang of "Gurren", and is able to generate drills of different sizes from the body. "Gurren Lagann" can also be further combined into an even more powerful mecha.
