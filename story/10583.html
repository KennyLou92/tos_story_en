Noriaki Kakyoin & Hierophant Green

A transfer student in the same high school as Jotaro. He was brainwashed by DIO during an earlier trip to Egypt. After Jotaro saved his life, he joined Jotaro on his quest to defeat DIO. He holds a strong devotion to his companions. He possesses Hierophant Green, a Stand which has the abilities of remote manipulation and Emerald Splash.
