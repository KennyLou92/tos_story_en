Gintoki Sakata

The leader of Yorozuya who does whatever the quest is. He has a strong sweet tooth that drives him crazy if he doesn’t have sweets regularly. Although he usually looks lethargic and weak, he is actually a great samurai. He is a former Jyoui-shishi and a highly-skilled samurai known as the terrifying “Shiroyasha”. But he only uses a wooden sword now.
