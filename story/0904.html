Demigoddess of Mana - Li Yi-Ru

“The lives of humans and the lives of Yaoguais — which is more important? If we can hunt Yaoguai to use as medicine to save Human lives, why can’t Yaoguais hunt Humans for food? Both strive solely to survive, right? Even though I’m Human, there’s also a part of me that isn’t Human...” Yi-ru believed that all beings were born with good natures. Regardless of whether it is a Human or a demonic creature, all were forced to harm others out of need. Because of this personality trait, when she was travelling through the martial world with Wang Hsiao-Hu, she captured many demonic creatures and alleviated many of the conflicts between her parents and her friends...
