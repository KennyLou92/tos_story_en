Littlegodzilla

Babygodzilla’s more mature appearance. As time passes, Babygodzilla absorbs Godzilla’s excess energies, which accelerates the youthful creature’s physical growth and development. Littlegodzilla can fire heatwaves from its mouth but they are still weak and foamy-looking. Raised for a time by humans, Littlegodzilla feels empathy and affection for them. When Spacegodzilla arrives on Birth Island, Littlegodzilla senses the creature’s connection to Godzilla and approaches it without fear. But when Spacegodzilla seals it inside a crystal chamber, the young monster’s eyes glow red.
